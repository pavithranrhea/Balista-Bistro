About Us

Crafting flavors and connections one dish at a time.

Our Story

Balista Bistro was born out of a spice-fueled accident in a small Brooklyn apartment. Co-founders Neha Kapoor and Arjun Patel, former food truck rivals turned unlikely friends, found themselves locked in during a blizzard with nothing but leftover tikka masala, stale sourdough, and a shared obsession with culinary chaos.

With no power and plenty of time, they grilled the sourdough on a makeshift stovetop and slapped on the tikka masala like it was a gourmet spread. That single, oddly perfect bite sparked a wild idea: what if Indian flavors met Western comfort food... without rules?

Our Mission & Values

Fusion Without Borders

We believe food should be fearless. We mix, mash, and experiment unapologetically — tradition meets innovation on every plate.

Community & Culture

We honor the roots of Indian cuisine while celebrating the diverse voices around our table. Everyone’s invited, and every story matters.

Do Good, Eat Well

From sourcing local ingredients to supporting food justice initiatives, we’re here to feed both people and purpose.
