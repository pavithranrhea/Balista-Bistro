Our Menu

Bold Indian flavors meet Western comfort food — no rules, just delicious. From spicy street snacks to fusion favorites, every dish brings a story to the table.

Salad and Meal bowl

Fresh, comforting, and full of flavor — our salads and bowls bring together wholesome ingredients with bold Indian twists. From herb-tossed greens to creamy lentils and vibrant burrito bowls, every option is crafted to satisfy and nourish.

Moon burgers

Pure vegetarian. Pure flavor. Our Moon Burgers bring the best of Indian street-style creativity to the classic burger — all 100% vegetarian. From the creamy Shahi Paneer to the spicy Chipotle kick, each bite is bold, satisfying, and full of desi flair.

Wraps - Culinary Sarongs

Wraps with a twist — and a whole lot of flavor. Our Culinary Sarongs are handcrafted vegetarian wraps packed with bold Indian spices, smoky tandoori flair, and fusion favorites. From street-style Bombay vibes to creamy paneer indulgence, every wrap is a portable taste adventure.

Beverages

Sip, slurp, and chill with our bold and refreshing beverage lineup. From fruity slushies and creamy shakes to chef-recommended Jamun Shots and classic Mango Lassi, every drink is crafted to cool you down and lift you up — all with a flavorful Indian twist.

Desserts

End on a sweet note with our indulgent, handcrafted treats. From warm walnut brownies to creamy ganache tarts and refreshing fruit sorbets, each dessert is a perfect blend of comfort and creativity — with a touch of Balista flair.
